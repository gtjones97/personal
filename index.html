About Me

I'm an IT specialist with almost 10 years of experience in the field and an academic background in computer science. I also freelance in web development, and I genuinely enjoy my work. My journey in tech has been driven by a passion for problem-solving and a love for learning.

In my current role, I manage all technology infrastructure for 17 library branches across seven counties in Georgia, including 200 Windows & Chrome computers, 100+ Google Workspace accounts, in addition to maintaining the library's website. Each library has its own dedicated network. I'm a help desk for the staff, I write grants, work alongside many vendors, and conduct cybersecurity training. I oversee a wide range of services, but I do my best to keep everything running smoothly.
